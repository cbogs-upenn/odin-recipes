Sour Cream Enchilada Casserole

Description

This is a dish I loved as a child and still love now. My mom got it out of a women's magazine in the 70s and it is my #1 comfort food to this day.

Ingredients

  • Cream of Chicken soup concentrate, 2 cans
  • Sour cream, 16 oz.
  • Monterrey jack cheese, 8 oz., grated
  • Green chiles, 8 oz.
  • Corn tortillas, 1 package
  • Cumin, 1 tsp.
  • Optional: black olives, 4 oz.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F.
  2. Reserve 2 oz. of the grated cheese for later.
  3. In a large bowl, mix soup, sour cream, the rest of the cheese, green chiles, optional olives, and the cumin until well mixed.
  4. Using a rubber spatula, spread a thin layer of this mixture in the bottom of an 11x14 baking dish.
  5. Tear the corn tortillas and make a layer of them in the bottom of the pan.
  6. Spread a layer of the mixture over these, and continue layering tortillas and the mixture until it is all used up. The top layer should be of the mixture.
  7. Sprinkle the reserved cheese over the top.
  8. Bake at 350F for 45 minutes or until top is golden brown.
  9. Cool and serve.
