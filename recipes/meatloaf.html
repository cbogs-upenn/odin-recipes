Meatloaf

Description

This meatloaf is made similarly to how my mom made it. The main difference between this recipe and most others that I am familiar with is that my mother always used oatmeal in her meatloaf. I prefer it this way, and it has the additional benefit of making the meatloaf gluten-free!

You can alter this recipe in many ways by adding ingredients to make your meatloaf more interesting. Onion, herbs and spices make excellent additions.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef, 2 lbs.
  • Oatmeal, 2 cups
  • Egg (1)
  • Tomato sauce, 8 oz.
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Onion, garlic, thyme, italian seasoning to taste

Steps

  1. Combine the meat, egg, oatmeal, spices, and 3/4 of the tomato sauce in a large bowl.
  2. Using your hands, mix the ingredients until well-blended. Do not overwork the mixture.
  3. Place the mixture into a loaf pan and round the top.
  4. Glaze the meatloaf with the remainder of the tomato sauce.
  5. Bake at 375F for one hour or until the internal temperature reaches 165F in the center.
  6. Remove from the oven and allow to cool to eating temperature. Serve sliced with ketchup and hot sauce.
